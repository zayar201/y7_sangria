About Us

EDUCode is a great website made to teach kids about HTML basics in a simple and engaging way.
You can learn the basics of HTML anytime, anywhere, with just this website.

By Group 1

EDUSN Y7 Sangria

EDUCode

We believe coding is for everyone,
and anyone can learn how to code anytime.

We are a dedicated team of EDUSN students, from Burma, who made this as a project for our WEB Class. We want everyone, younger or older to learn coding as it is valuable for their later life.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission and vision are to focus on giving more exciting job opportunities for children in the future. We believe that everyone can succeed in their lessons and even more from advice, tips, and tricks. With a foundation of knowledge and skills, we aim to open pathways to fulfilling careers and a promising future.

We are dedicated to ensuring that children not only excel academically but also acquire the practical insights to success in various fields. Through advice, effective tips, and proven strategies, we want to build a supportive environment where every child can unlock their true potential and succeed beyond their expectations.
